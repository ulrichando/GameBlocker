Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

ParentShield ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our parental control software.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us:

  • Account information (email, name) when you subscribe
  • Payment information processed securely by Stripe
  • Support requests and communications

Information Collected by the Software

ParentShield is designed with privacy in mind:

  • All blocking rules and configurations are stored locally on your device
  • We do NOT collect browsing history or personal data from protected devices
  • We do NOT transmit activity logs to our servers
  • Master passwords are hashed locally and never transmitted

How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To process your subscription payments
  • To send important updates about the software
  • To respond to your support requests

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Payment processing is handled by Stripe, a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor.

Children's Privacy

ParentShield is designed for use by parents to protect their children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Contact us at privacy@parentshield.app to exercise these rights.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@parentshield.app
