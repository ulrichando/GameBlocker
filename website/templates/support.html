How Can We Help?

Find answers to common questions or get in touch with our support team

How do I install ParentShield?

Download the installer for your operating system, run it with administrator privileges, and follow the setup wizard. You'll be asked to create a master password during installation.

I forgot my master password. What do I do?

For security reasons, the master password cannot be recovered. You'll need to uninstall and reinstall ParentShield, which will reset all settings. This ensures children cannot bypass the protection.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime from your account settings. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the billing period. Contact support@parentshield.app if you need assistance.

Does ParentShield work with VPNs?

ParentShield includes VPN detection and blocking capabilities. Enable "Block VPN Ports" in Settings > Advanced to prevent VPN-based bypasses.

How many devices can I protect?

Your subscription covers unlimited devices within your family. Install ParentShield on as many Windows, Mac, and Linux computers as you need.
